Bio

Juan is a computer scientist, engineer, and entrepreneur. He is obsessed with knowledge. Currently, he is building tools to assist Knowledge distribution (learning) and discovery (science). Previously, he cofounded and tech-led Loki Studios, an augmented reality mobile gaming company acquired by Yahoo; and worked with StartX as an EIR. Juan studied Computer Science at Stanford University (BS 2010, MS on leave).
